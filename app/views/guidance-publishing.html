Publishing data

[What does this page look like without references to the repo?]

Authorities are encouraged to specify how they own data that is commissioned so that it can be made open and shared on oxcam.repo.co.uk

You must state on your web page that the data is provided under the Open Government Licence.

Data collected and/or commissioned by Local Authorities can be uploaded on oxcam.repo.co.uk

It is the responsibility of the Authority who owns the data to keep the data updated on oxcam.repo.co.uk.
