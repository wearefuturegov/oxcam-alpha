Guidance

Publishing data

We encourage you to specify how you own data that is commissioned, so it can be made open and shared on Find Evidence Base Data.

You must state on your web page that the data is provided under the Open Government Licence.

Data collected and/or commissioned by Local Authorities can be uploaded on Find Evidence Base Data.

It is the responsibility of the Authority who owns the data to keep the data updated on Find Evidence Base Data.
